# AI-PPT
A PowerPoint presentation exploring the applications and impact of Artificial Intelligence in everyday life.
AI in Daily Life
Overview

This project contains a PowerPoint presentation on Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Daily Life. The presentation highlights how AI is integrated into everyday activities and its role in improving convenience, efficiency, and decision-making.

Topics Covered
Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
AI in Smartphones and Virtual Assistants
AI in Social Media and Entertainment
AI in Healthcare
AI in Education
AI in Transportation
Benefits and Challenges of AI
Future Scope of AI
Objective

The objective of this presentation is to provide a basic understanding of AI and demonstrate its real-world applications in daily life.
